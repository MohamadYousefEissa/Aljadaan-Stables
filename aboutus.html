The Pure Arabian Horses culture. Arabs in Syria consider horses as their companions on the long journey from 
past to present.
This precious part of our culture has shaped the nature and personalities of Syrians.
As a part of the National Program for caring, preserving, increasing and promoting the Arabian Horses, Al Jadaan Stables of Arabian Horses was established in Damascus. With many public and private stables Horse. Al Jadaan Stables of Arabian Horses was established in Damascus. With many public and private stables, Al Jadaan Stables has done its utmost to protect the pure Arabian Syrian Horse and to promote its importance in local and international fields. Furthermore Al Jadaan Stables has taken steps to present the Syrian Arabian Horses as a unique group worldwide.

These Syrian Arabian Horses still maintain their physical and mental characteristics tracing their descent to the Arabian Tribes in the Sham desert.

These tribes kept these horses during their long history and carried the name of their individuals and clans.

Our aim is to reinstate the purity of the Syrian Arabian Horse. This stems from our country's growing interest in its horses, their history and culture. After all those who brush tradition to one side, will have no part in the future development of Syria and the Arabian Horse.
